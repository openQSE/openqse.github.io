Overview

The openQSE Initiative is organizing a workshop co-located with IEEE Quantum Week 2026 (QCE26). This event brings together vendors, research institutions, national labs, and universities to advance the open quantum-HPC software ecosystem.

Topics include open quantum software standards, cross-platform interoperability, quantum-HPC integration, and community-driven tooling.
